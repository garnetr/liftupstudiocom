* Food along the way, $20 per day, $400 total

* AAA membership $100
* My 2010 Grand Caravan SXT with 330K miles on it seems in good shape, but could their be car repairs along the way?
* Total is close to $5000

I can fund this trip by stopping along the way to sharpen scissors all across the country. I am willing to do this.
